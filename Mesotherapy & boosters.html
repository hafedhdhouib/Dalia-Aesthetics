Mesotherapy & boosters

Mesotherapy engages painless micro-injections of vitamins, minerals, besides hyaluronic and amino acids, the technique uses very fine needles to deliver a series of injections into the middle layer (mesoderm) of skin. The idea behind mesotherapy is that it corrects underlying issues like poor circulation and inflammation that cause skin damage. There isn’t a standard formula for the substances injected in mesotherapy. Doctors use many different solutions, like manual injections, nano-pore micro-needling, gold stamp, and meso-gun. Skin Boosters line brings you the revolution in cosmetology science and assures our commitment to providing you with high-end, top-quality products, capable of maximising efficacy with a safe and natural way. The characteristic advantage of the line is that its products are in fact “skin boosters”: products with dynamic, complex and technologically advanced formulas, which boost and enhance the natural processes and functions of the skin. Skin boosters are the bolus dose of concentrated ingredients given to the client via injectable & non injectable technique that helps the aging and damaged skin to return to their normal function over 30-60 days. The booster’s main ingredients are high concentration of hyaluronic acid, growth factors and amino acids that correct the structure of the dermis and give proper hydration.
