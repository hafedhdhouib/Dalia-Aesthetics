Acne & post acne spots

Acne is perhaps one of the most common skin problems, no matter where you live in the world. It affects almost everyone at some stage in life. However, it is especially troublesome during adolescence when the hormones are fluctuating. Therefore, a timely and effective Acne Treatment is essential. Otherwise, untreated acne can cause breakouts that can lead to pain as well as scarring. Before discussing the best acne treatment, let’s understand its causes and how to avoid them.
What causes acne?
Acne is caused by the clogging of skin pores. It generally happens when the skin produces excessive natural oil, called sebum. The excess oil produced by sebaceous glands, along with dirt and grime, gets trapped in open pores and causes acne. Therefore, you should always cleanse and moisturize your facial skin with a gentle moisturizer to prevent acne.

Treatment Options
There are numerous options for Acne and Acne Scars Treatment. The most commonly used treatments include topical treatments, deep pores cleansing, blue light therapy, chemical peels, and laser skin resurfacing. However, the acne treatment price in Dubai varies for each treatment.
Topical Treatments
Topical applications are generally the first defense against acne. They work by killing bacteria and unclogging pores. Retinoids and antibiotic creams are commonly used to get rid of pimples. However, topical treatments are effective for mild to moderate acne. In rare cases, the doctor may opt for isotretinoin. Although it works well when no other treatment is taken, it can sometimes have severe side effects. Chemical peels are also an effective Acne Removal Treatment in Dubai. These acidic solutions are applied to the skin and left in place for some time before wiping off. Moreover, chemical peels can also be used as an effective Acne Scars Treatment to treat mild to moderate acne scarring.
